Projects

Mum & Baby

I developed the Mum & Baby app, a comprehensive maternity care tool in partnership with the NHS, which has scaled from 5k to over 50k downloads, showcasing my ability to handle large-scale, maintainable projects. The app guides users from pregnancy to early parenthood by allowing them to set due dates, explore nearby maternity units, self-refer for care, track appointments, access personalized plans, and view local contacts. It has achieved the highest rating of 86% from ORCHA for its quality content, regularly updated by an approved panel of clinicians.

The Organised Mum

I currently maintain The Organised Mum app, which has garnered over 10k downloads and has become a go-to solution for users looking to streamline household chores. The app is based on The Organised Mum Method (TOMM), a globally popular cleaning routine. It helps users efficiently manage housework with customizable task lists, guided cleaning sessions synced with their music, and meal plans to keep daily routines on track. The app's focus on balancing chores with more free time has received rave reviews for its ease of use and effectiveness

Dued App

I maintain the DUED APP, a cloud-based due diligence defense system designed for real-time remote monitoring. Recognized as one of the top solutions by enforcement authorities across the UK, the app provides businesses with a reliable and efficient way to manage compliance while also contributing to environmental sustainability. The DUED APP ensures comprehensive due diligence practices are in place, offering an intuitive interface for tracking and monitoring compliance activities, helping users stay ahead of regulatory requirements.
